The Dish on Dish

At Dish, we have a passion for good food, travel and design. Whether we're on the lookout for the crispiest mulita in LA, or the most authentic carbonara in NY, one thing is certain: Life is too short to eat bad food. And living in Los Angeles, there’s definitely no shortage of great food. From Guatemalan, Lebanese, Szechuan to tasting menus, pork collar and vegan (yes, we even have pretty good vegan), there’s more amazing food than there are Teslas on the road (sorry, Elon).


Our problem is not finding great food, it’s keeping track of, organizing and remembering all of the places we like. Most of us travel a lot, too, which makes our problem exponentially worse. It’s hard enough to remember all of the hole in the wall spots across LA’s sprawl, but now try adding Mexico City into the mix!


Until now, every food app in existence has been built on the premise that it needs to tell you where to eat. Or we should say, “its advertisers” need to tell you where to eat. And as we all know, if they have to pay for ads, the food can’t be good. And if you are anything like us, not only do you know the places you like, but you already have trusted sources for finding new places.


Dish has completely turned the concept of a food app inside out. Instead of thinking about a food app as an advertising platform (or “pay to play” review site), Dish has created the first food app for foodies.


Oh, The Places You'll Go!
